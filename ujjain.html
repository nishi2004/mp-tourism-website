Best Time to Visit Ujjain

  • October to March: Pleasant weather (15°C to 30°C), perfect for sightseeing and attending festivals like Navratri and Kartik Mela.
  • Mahashivratri (March): A vibrant festival at Mahakaleshwar Temple, ideal for spiritual seekers.
  • Simhastha Kumbh Mela (every 12 years, next in 2028): A rare, grand event at Shipra River, attracting millions.
  • Avoid April to June: Hot and humid (35°C to 45°C), less comfortable for outdoor activities.
