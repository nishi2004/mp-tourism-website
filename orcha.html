Best Time to Visit Orchha

  • October to March: Pleasant weather (10°C to 27°C), ideal for exploring forts, temples, and attending festivals.
  • Orchha Festival (March): A vibrant celebration of music and dance at Jahangir Mahal.
  • Diwali (October-November): Festive lights and cultural events at Chaturbhuj Temple.
  • Avoid April to June: Hot and dry (30°C to 45°C), making outdoor sightseeing challenging.
