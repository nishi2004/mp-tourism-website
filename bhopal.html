Best Time to Visit Bhopal

  • October to March: Cool and comfortable (10°C to 25°C), ideal for exploring lakes, museums, and festivals.
  • Bhopal Literature Festival (January): A literary celebration at Bharat Bhavan, perfect for book lovers.
  • Lokrang Festival (January): A cultural extravaganza showcasing tribal arts at Ravindra Bhavan.
  • Avoid April to June: Hot and dry (30°C to 40°C), with occasional heatwaves, less suitable for outdoor activities.
