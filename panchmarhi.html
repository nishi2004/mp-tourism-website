Best Time to Visit Pachmarhi

  • October to March: Pleasant weather (10°C to 25°C), perfect for trekking, sightseeing, and enjoying waterfalls.
  • Monsoon (July to September): Lush landscapes and misty views, ideal for a tranquil escape (avoid heavy rains).
  • Pachmarhi Summer Festival (May): A cultural event with local performances and outdoor activities.
  • Avoid April to June: Hot (25°C to 35°C), less comfortable due to pre-monsoon heat.
