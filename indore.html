Best Time to Visit Indore

Indore, famed for its food and vibrant culture, is best visited during the cooler months or during key festivals. Enjoy the pleasant weather and culinary delights at their peak.

  • October to March: Mild weather (15°C to 28°C), perfect for food trails, sightseeing, and festivals.
  • Indore Food Festival (December): A gastronomic delight showcasing local cuisine at Sarafa Bazaar.
  • Diwali Mela (October): Festive markets and celebrations at Yeshwant Club.
  • Avoid April to June: Hot and humid (32°C to 42°C), challenging for outdoor exploration.
